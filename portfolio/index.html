About

After many years in the hospitality industry I decided to change career. Coding is something I was considering in the past and it was a great opportunity to look into that further. After spending a few months in self-teaching I decided to enroll in Flatiron's course called Learn. During this 7 month period of intensive training I fell deeper in love with the process of problem solving, making connections, and creating things from scratch.

This portfolio showcases my work at Flatiron as well as ongoing projects. Currently I m looking for opportunities where I can put my knowledge into practice and continue to learn new things.
